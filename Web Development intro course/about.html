System Of A Down

ABOUT

Where else would a band start than an Amermian School? Yes, you heard that right System of a Down started when two bandmates at Rose and Alex Palebos Amerinian School in Los Angeles, California. The union of singer Serj Tankian and guitarist Daron Malakian would eventually lead to one of the legendary bands to be formed in this century. The name "System of a Down" came from a poem Malakian had written titled "Victims of a Down"; however, the word "victims" was changed to "system" to appeal to a wider audience and to have their records be alphabetically be shelved closer to their musical heroes, Slayer. Be sure to check out absolute bangers of Albums such as "System of a Down", "Toxicity", or even "Mezmerize"! All wonderful listened by the creative battlefield that is the band.
